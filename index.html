Together we protect the Web3 world and the Security of Crypto assets

IntroductionProject Context

As Web3 continues to evolve at pace, the onchain ecosystem is flourishing, with rising numbers of users participating in onchain transactions and other activities. Simultaneously, various onchain scams are emerging, with fraudsters tricking users into authorizing transactions or revealing their keys to steal assets.

In April 2023, we launched the USDT BlackList project, which was originally established to help Web3 users avoid on-chain fraud/phishing, etc. In order to help you identify and avoid Web3 risks.

Today, we are moving from centralized operations to decentralized ones.The goal is to get more individuals and organizations involved and work with us to maintain the security of the Web3 world.

GovernanceWeb3 Security DAO

Through a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO), we will establish a Web3 security community. We call it Web3 Security DAO (W3SD). Any individual/company/organization can apply to become a DAO member. Its vision is to allow all members to jointly serve as a management body.

DAO can achieve multiple purposes, including members can submit risky addresses and clues to the community (contribution), members can verify the authenticity of data together (voting), avoid abuse of power, and members who contribute valuable contributions can receive rewards, etc.
